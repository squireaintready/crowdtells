# Impeccable design context — Crowdtells

## Design Context

### Users
News readers who want to know what's worth paying attention to and what the crowd
(markets + community) believes about it — now and over time. They read on phones and
desktops, often in short sessions; a secondary audience follows specific stories over
days/weeks via story pages, digests, and breaking alerts. The job to be done: "brief me
on the stories that matter, show me the crowd's read, and keep an honest record of it."

### Brand Personality
Editorial, accountable, quietly confident. A broadsheet newsroom that happens to read
the tape — journalism leads, market data is the "why this matters." Three words:
**editorial · trustworthy · timeless**. Anti-personality: crypto-trading dashboard,
neon fintech, hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
"Ink on paper" editorial system (see `src/styles/tokens.css`): warm paper light theme
by default, two opt-in dark themes (bordeaux, forest) with antique-brass accents.
Hairline rules over shadows, print-like near-square radii, one restrained ink-blue
accent in light. Type: Fraunces (serif voice), Source Serif 4 (display), Inter (UI
sans), mono for figures. These are settled, deliberate choices — audits should enforce
consistency *with this system*, not replace it.

Established house rules:
- Left-side accent highlights must gradient-fade from their vertical center — never a
  solid bar (owner rule, shipped in `441b0d0`).
- Masthead pun stays: "The crowd tells it first." Hero promise: "A living record of
  what the crowd believes."
- Lead with the living-record moat, not "prediction markets."

### Design Principles
1. **Journalism leads.** Market data supports the story; never let odds/volume UI
   shout over the reporting.
2. **Editorial restraint.** Rules and rhythm over shadows and glow; the accent is rare
   on purpose.
3. **The record must read honestly.** Timelines, Calls, and scores are the product —
   clarity and legibility beat decoration everywhere they appear.
4. **Every surface works on a phone.** Short-session mobile reading is the primary
   context.
5. **Consistency with tokens.** All color/spacing/type flows through
   `src/styles/tokens.css`; hard-coded values are defects.
